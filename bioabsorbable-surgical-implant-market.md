<p><ul><li>Synthetic Material</li><li>Natural Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bioabsorbable surgical implant market is categorized into two main types: synthetic and natural materials. Synthetic materials include polymers like polylactic acid and polyglycolic acid, which are engineered for specific applications and provide controlled degradation rates. Natural materials, on the other hand, are derived from biological sources, such as collagen and chitosan, offering biocompatibility and reduced immunogenicity. Both types serve critical roles in medical procedures, promoting healing while gradually absorbing into the body, minimizing the need for second surgeries.</p></p>
