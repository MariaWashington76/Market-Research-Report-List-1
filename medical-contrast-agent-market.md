<p><p>The medical contrast agent market encompasses various types, each serving distinct imaging needs. Barium-based contrast media are primarily used in gastrointestinal imaging, enhancing radiographic studies. Iodinated contrast media are vital for X-rays and CT scans, providing excellent visibility of blood vessels and organs. Gadolinium-based contrast agents are essential in MRI scans, improving soft tissue imaging. Microbubble contrast media are utilized in ultrasound imaging to enhance vascular delineation, offering real-time insights into blood flow and perfusion.</p></p>
