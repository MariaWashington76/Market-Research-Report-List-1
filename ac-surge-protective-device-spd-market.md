<p><p>The AC Surge Protective Device (SPD) market is categorized into two main types: solid-state SPDs and hybrid SPDs. Solid-state AC SPDs are composed of electronic components that provide rapid response to voltage surges, ensuring high reliability and durability. In contrast, hybrid AC SPDs combine both solid-state technology and traditional components like metal oxide varistors (MOVs), offering enhanced protection by leveraging the strengths of both systems. This combination results in improved performance against various surge conditions, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
