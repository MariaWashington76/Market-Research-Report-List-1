<p><strong>The Automatic Weighchecker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Checkweighers</li><li>Intermittent Checkweighers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic weighchecker market consists of two main types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously measure the weight of products as they move along production lines, ensuring real-time monitoring and enhancing efficiency. Conversely, intermittent checkweighers operate by stopping products briefly to assess their weight, often providing greater accuracy but reducing throughput. Both types play crucial roles in quality control across various industries, ensuring compliance with weight regulations and minimizing product variances.</p></p>
