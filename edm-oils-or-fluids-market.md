<p><p>EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) oils or fluids are specialized dielectric liquids used in the EDM process, crucial for providing cooling and lubrication during the machining of metallic components. These fluids facilitate efficient electrical discharges between the tool and workpiece, enhancing the precision and quality of the machining process. The market for EDM oils is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in various manufacturing sectors, particularly aerospace, automotive, and electronics, where precision machining is paramount.</p><p>The EDM Oils or Fluids Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. Innovations in formulation that enhance thermal stability and reduce electrode wear are also pivotal in shaping market dynamics.
