<p><strong>The PEM Electrolysis Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxygen Evolution Reaction (OER) Catalysts</li><li>Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER) Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PEM electrolysis catalysts market is primarily categorized into two types: Oxygen Evolution Reaction (OER) catalysts and Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER) catalysts. OER catalysts facilitate the oxidation of water molecules to produce oxygen, playing a crucial role in improving the efficiency of the electrolysis process. Conversely, HER catalysts promote the reduction of protons to generate hydrogen. Both catalyst types are essential for optimizing performance, enhancing stability, and minimizing costs in the production of green hydrogen through PEM electrolysis.</p></p>
