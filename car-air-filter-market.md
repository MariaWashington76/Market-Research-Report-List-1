<p><p>The car air filter market includes two main types: **Paper** and **Oil Bath**. **Paper air filters** are disposable, made of pleated paper, and trap dust and debris efficiently. They are lightweight, cost-effective, and widely used in modern vehicles. **Oil Bath air filters** use an oil-coated mesh to capture contaminants, requiring periodic cleaning and re-oiling. They are durable and suited for heavy-duty or off-road vehicles. Both types ensure clean air intake, improving engine performance and longevity, but differ in maintenance and application.</p></p>
