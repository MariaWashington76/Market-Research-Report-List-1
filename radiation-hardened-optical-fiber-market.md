<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber</li><li>Multimode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation-hardened optical fiber market is divided into two main types: single mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single mode fiber features a small core, allowing light to travel straight down the fiber with minimal loss, making it ideal for long-distance communication in radiation-prone environments. Multimode fiber, with a larger core, allows multiple light modes to propagate, suitable for shorter distances and applications requiring high bandwidth. Both types are essential for ensuring reliable data transmission in harsh conditions, such as space or nuclear facilities.</p></p>
