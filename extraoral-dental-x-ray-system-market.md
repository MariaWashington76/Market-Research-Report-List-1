Panoramic X-ray units provide a single image capturing the entire dental arch, useful for assessing tooth positioning and detecting abnormalities. Cephalometric X-ray units are used for orthodontic analysis by capturing the lateral view of the skull. CBCT offers 3D imaging for detailed inspection of dental structures, enhancing diagnostic capabilities and treatment planning in dentistry.</p></p>
