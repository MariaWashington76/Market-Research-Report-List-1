<p><p>The saffron market can be categorized into three main types: thread form, powder form, and liquid form. Thread form consists of whole saffron strands, prized for their potency and aroma, often used in gourmet cooking and traditional medicines. Powder form offers convenience for culinary applications but may vary in quality and authenticity. Liquid form, typically an extract, provides a concentrated solution for easy incorporation into dishes and beverages. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and culinary needs, impacting market dynamics.</p></p>
