<p><p>The bio-based (eco) fertilizer market encompasses various types that enhance soil health and nutrient availability. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers capture atmospheric nitrogen, enriching the soil and benefiting crops. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers convert insoluble phosphates into bioavailable forms, promoting root development. Potash-mobilizing fertilizers enhance potassium availability, essential for plant growth and stress resistance. Additionally, other categories may include bio-stimulants or microbial inoculants that improve overall soil fertility and plant health. Collectively, these products support sustainable agriculture and reduce chemical inputs.</p></p>
