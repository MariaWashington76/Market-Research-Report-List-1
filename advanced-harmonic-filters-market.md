<p><p>The advanced harmonic filters market consists of three main types: passive filters, active filters, and hybrid filters. Passive filters use inductors and capacitors to mitigate harmonics but may be less efficient at varying loads. Active filters dynamically adjust to changing conditions, providing real-time compensation for harmonic distortion. Hybrid filters combine features of both, offering the benefits of passive and active systems for greater flexibility and efficiency. These filters play a crucial role in improving power quality across various industrial applications.</p></p>
