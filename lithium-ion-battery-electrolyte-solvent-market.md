<p><p>The lithium-ion battery electrolyte solvent market consists of various types, including Ethylene Carbonate (EC), Diethyl Carbonate (DEC), Dimethyl Carbonate (DMC), Ethyl Methyl Carbonate (EMC), and Propylene Carbonate (PC). EC is widely used for its high dielectric constant and ability to dissolve lithium salts. DEC and DMC are favored for their low viscosity and volatility, enhancing battery performance. EMC improves thermal stability, while PC offers excellent ionic conductivity. Others in the market encompass alternative solvents catering to specific performance requirements.</p></p>
