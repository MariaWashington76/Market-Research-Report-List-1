<p><p>The automotive electric shock absorber market includes several key types. Conventional telescopic shock absorbers use a cylindrical design to control vehicle movement. Strut type shock absorbers integrate the spring and damper into a single unit, enhancing structural integrity and saving space. Spring seat shock absorbers feature a spring mounted on the shock itself, providing a compact design and efficient performance. These technologies contribute to improved ride quality, handling, and overall vehicle stability, catering to diverse automotive applications and consumer needs.</p></p>
