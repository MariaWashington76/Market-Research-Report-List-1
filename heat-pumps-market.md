<p><p>The heat pump market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Air to Water Monobloc systems combine all components in a single outdoor unit, making installation easier. Air to Water Cylinders utilize a separate cylinder for storing heated water. Ground/Water to Water systems extract heat from the ground or water bodies, offering high efficiency. Air to Water Split systems feature separate units for indoor and outdoor, enhancing flexibility. Exhaust Air systems recycle heat from indoor air, maximizing energy efficiency.</p></p>
