<p><p>The adhesion barrier market includes three primary formulations: film, gel, and liquid. Film formulations create a solid layer that separates tissues, preventing adhesions post-surgery. Gel formulations provide a viscous solution that conforms to tissue surfaces, allowing easy application while minimizing friction. Liquid formulations offer a spray or solution that can penetrate intricate areas, ensuring uniform coverage and protection. Each type serves specific surgical needs, enhancing patient outcomes by reducing the risk of post-operative adhesions and complications.</p></p>
