<p><p>The automobile plate spring assembly market includes multi-leaf and little-leaf springs, both used in vehicle suspension systems. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple steel layers stacked together, offering high load-bearing capacity and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and buses. Little-leaf springs, with fewer and thinner leaves, provide a smoother ride and are commonly used in lighter vehicles such as cars and SUVs. Both types balance performance, weight, and cost, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
