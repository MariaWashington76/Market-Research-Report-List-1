<p><p>The packaged condensed milk market primarily consists of two types: sweetened condensed milk and evaporated milk. Sweetened condensed milk is a thick, sweet product made by removing water from milk and adding sugar, commonly used in desserts and beverages. In contrast, evaporated milk is produced through the evaporation of water from milk without added sugar, offering a creamy texture and serving as a versatile ingredient in cooking and baking. Both types cater to diverse culinary needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
