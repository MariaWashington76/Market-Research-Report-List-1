<p><p>The cardiology defibrillator devices market comprises three main types: Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), which are surgically placed within the body to monitor and correct life-threatening arrhythmias; External Defibrillators, which are portable devices used in emergencies to deliver electric shocks for resuscitation; and Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs), designed for patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest that can be worn externally to monitor heart rhythms and provide shocks if needed. Each type serves critical roles in cardiac care and management.</p></p>
