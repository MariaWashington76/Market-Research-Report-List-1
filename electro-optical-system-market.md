<p><p>The Electro Optical System market is primarily categorized into two types: Infrared and Laser systems. Infrared systems utilize thermal imaging to detect heat emitted from objects, making them ideal for surveillance and night vision applications. Conversely, Laser systems employ focused beams of light for precision targeting, range-finding, and high-resolution imaging. Both types are integral to defense, aerospace, and industrial sectors, providing enhanced visibility and accuracy in various operational environments. Their respective technologies cater to distinct functionalities and user requirements.</p></p>
