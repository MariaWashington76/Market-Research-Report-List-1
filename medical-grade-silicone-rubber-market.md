<p><ul><li>High Consistency Rubber (HCR)</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade silicone rubber market comprises two primary types: High Consistency Rubber (HCR) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HCR is characterized by its solid form, offering excellent durability and heat resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring strong, robust materials. On the other hand, LSR is flowable, allowing for intricate mold designs and precise detail, ideal for complex medical devices. Both types are biocompatible and play crucial roles in various medical applications, including implants, tubing, and surgical instruments.</p></p>
