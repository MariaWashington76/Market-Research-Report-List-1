<p><p>Peripheral artery revascularization encompasses various procedures aimed at restoring blood flow in affected arteries. Lower extremity revascularization targets leg arteries to alleviate symptoms of peripheral artery disease. Renal arterial revascularization focuses on kidneys to treat hypertension or ischemia. Mesenteric arterial revascularization addresses blood flow to intestines, improving digestion and nutrient absorption. Abdominal aortic revascularization involves procedures around the abdominal aorta to enhance overall lower body circulation. The "others" category includes additional vascular interventions not classified in these primary types.</p></p>
