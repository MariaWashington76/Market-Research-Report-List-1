<p><p>The medical implants sterile packaging market encompasses various types designed to protect and maintain the sterility of medical devices. Pouches and bags are flexible containers that seal implants securely. Clamshell packaging consists of two hinged halves, offering easy visibility and access. Blister packaging features a plastic bubble that holds the implant, providing robust protection. Tubes are cylindrical containers often used for smaller implants, while vials are sealed glass or plastic containers for liquid implants. Other packaging types may include specialized formats tailored to specific implant needs.</p></p>
