<p><strong>The Traction Elevators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geared Traction Elevators</li><li>Gearless Traction Elevators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traction elevators are classified into two main types: geared and gearless elevators. Geared traction elevators utilize a gearbox to control the motor's speed, making them suitable for mid-rise buildings due to their cost-effectiveness and reliability. In contrast, gearless traction elevators operate without gears, providing smoother rides and higher efficiency, ideal for high-rise buildings. Both types leverage ropes and pulleys for movement, offering advantages in energy efficiency, load capacity, and maintenance, catering to various building requirements and heights.</p></p>
