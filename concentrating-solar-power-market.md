<p><ul><li>Parabolic Trough</li><li>Solar Tower</li><li>Linear Fresnel</li><li>Dish/Engine Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concentrating Solar Power (CSP) technology harnesses solar energy through various market types. Parabolic trough systems use curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver tube, generating steam for electricity. Solar towers employ a field of mirrors directing sunlight to a central tower, achieving high temperatures for power generation. Linear Fresnel systems utilize flat mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver, while dish/engine systems combine parabolic dishes with engines to convert concentrated solar heat into mechanical power. Each type offers distinct efficiencies and applications.</p></p>
