<p><strong>The Thick Layer Photoresists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresists</li><li>Negative Photoresists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thick layer photoresists market encompasses two main types: positive and negative photoresists. Positive photoresists become soluble where exposed to light, allowing for the creation of detailed patterns during the manufacturing process. In contrast, negative photoresists harden upon exposure, leading to the unexposed areas being removed. Both types are essential in various applications, including semiconductor fabrication and microfabrication, offering unique advantages based on specific production requirements and design complexities. Their selection is crucial for optimizing performance and quality in precision engineering.</p></p>
