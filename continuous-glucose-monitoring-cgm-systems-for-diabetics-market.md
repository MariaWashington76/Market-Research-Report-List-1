<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems are advanced medical devices designed to assist diabetics in managing their blood glucose levels. <p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems for diabetics are crucial for managing blood sugar levels. The two primary market types are wearable and implantable CGMs. Wearable devices, such as patches or sensors placed on the skin's surface, offer convenient, non-invasive monitoring. In contrast, implantable CGMs are surgically placed under the skin for long-term glucose tracking, providing more stable readings and reducing the need for frequent replacements. Both types enhance diabetes management through real-time data and improved patient insights.</p></p>
