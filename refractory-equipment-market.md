<p><p>The refractory equipment market includes various technologies for processing materials utilized in high-temperature applications. Impact mills are designed for crushing and grinding with high efficiency, while air separators optimize particle size distribution by using air flow. Pendulum roller mills provide fine milling through swinging rollers, ensuring uniformity. Ring-roll mills excel in producing fine powders from hard materials, making them ideal for refractory applications. Together, these types enhance the production and performance of refractory materials in various industries.</p></p>
