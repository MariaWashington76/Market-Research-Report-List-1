<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensors</li><li>Flow Meters</li><li>Fibre Optic Sensors</li><li>Acoustic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil pipeline leak detection system market comprises several key technologies. Pressure sensors monitor pressure changes within pipelines, indicating potential leaks. Flow meters assess the volume of fluid moving through the pipeline, identifying discrepancies that may signal leaks. Fibre optic sensors utilize light transmission to detect changes in temperature or pressure along the pipeline. Acoustic sensors listen for sound waves generated by leaks, providing real-time monitoring capabilities. These technologies are essential for enhancing safety and minimizing environmental impact in the oil industry.</p></p>
