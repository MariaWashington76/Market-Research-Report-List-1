<p><p>The natural colorants market is categorized into three main types: plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based colorants. Animal-derived colorants, although less common, include pigments from sources like insects and shellfish, often used in cosmetics and textiles. Mineral colorants, extracted from naturally occurring minerals, provide stable and intense colors, widely utilized in food, cosmetics, and artistic applications, appealing to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
