<p><strong>The SMT-SPI System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line SPI System</li><li>Off-line SPI System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMT-SPI (Solder Paste Inspection) system market comprises two main types: in-line SPI systems and off-line SPI systems. In-line SPI systems are integrated into the production line, providing real-time monitoring and feedback during the PCB assembly process, enhancing efficiency and reducing defects. Off-line SPI systems, on the other hand, are standalone units used for batch inspection after solder paste application, allowing for detailed analysis and adjustment of processes without interrupting ongoing production. Both types aim to ensure quality and reliability in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
