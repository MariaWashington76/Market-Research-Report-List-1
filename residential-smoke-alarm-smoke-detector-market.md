<p><p>The residential smoke alarm market features three main types: Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light beam to detect smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization smoke alarms contain radioactive material that senses rapid flames, responding quickly to fast-burning fires. Combination smoke alarms integrate both technologies, offering enhanced protection by detecting a wider range of fire types. These varied options cater to differing residential needs, emphasizing safety and reliability in smoke detection for homeowners.</p></p>
