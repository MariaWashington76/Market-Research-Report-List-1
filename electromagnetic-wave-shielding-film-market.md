<p><p>The electromagnetic wave shielding film market comprises three main types: Conductive Adhesive Type, Metal Alloy Type, and Metal Microneedle Type. Conductive Adhesive Type films utilize conductive materials combined with adhesives to effectively block electromagnetic interference. Metal Alloy Type films incorporate metal alloys for enhanced durability and shielding performance. Metal Microneedle Type films feature microneedles that penetrate surfaces, providing a unique approach to shielding by creating an effective barrier against electromagnetic waves while maintaining flexibility and adhesion properties.</p></p>
