<p><strong>The Immunochromato Test Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Method</li><li>Fluorescence Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromatography test reader market primarily consists of two main types: absorption method and fluorescence method. The absorption method relies on visual detection of color changes caused by the binding of antigens to antibodies on a test strip, making it simple and cost-effective. In contrast, the fluorescence method utilizes fluorescent markers to enhance sensitivity and specificity, allowing for clearer results even at lower analyte concentrations. Both methods are widely adopted in clinical diagnostics, providing valuable tools for rapid testing and disease detection.</p></p>
