<p><ul><li>Manual Transmission Fluids</li><li>Automatic Transmission Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle transmission fluids market comprises two primary types: manual transmission fluids and automatic transmission fluids. Manual transmission fluids are designed for vehicles with stick-shift systems, providing lubrication and facilitating smooth gear shifting. In contrast, automatic transmission fluids are formulated for vehicles with automatic transmissions, offering cooling, cleaning, and friction management to ensure optimal performance. Both types are essential for enhancing the durability and efficiency of commercial vehicles, addressing the specific needs of various transmission systems.</p></p>
