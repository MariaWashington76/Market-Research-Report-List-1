<p><p>A Data Management System (DBMS) is software that facilitates the storage, retrieval, and management of data in databases. The market is divided into relational and non-relational categories. Relational DBMS (RDBMS) uses structured data in tables and supports SQL for queries, ideal for applications requiring strong consistency. Non-relational DBMS, or NoSQL, accommodates unstructured data and scales horizontally, making it suitable for big data and real-time applications. Each type caters to specific business needs and data handling requirements.</p></p>
