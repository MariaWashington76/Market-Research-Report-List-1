<p><p>The blood pressure monitoring equipment market features two primary types: dynamic blood pressure monitors and ordinary blood pressure monitors. Dynamic blood pressure monitors, also known as ambulatory monitors, provide continuous pressure readings over 24 hours, offering a comprehensive view of blood pressure fluctuations throughout daily activities. In contrast, ordinary blood pressure monitors, which include both digital and manual devices, offer single measurements at specific times, making them widely accessible for home use. Both types cater to varying patient needs and healthcare settings.</p></p>
