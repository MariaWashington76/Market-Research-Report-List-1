<p><p>The Industrial Edge Controller market consists of two primary types: DIN-rail and Panel Mount. DIN-rail controllers are designed for easy installation within standard rail systems, making them ideal for industrial environments with limited space. They offer flexibility and modularity. Panel Mount controllers, on the other hand, are integrated directly onto control panels, providing a streamlined solution for operators. Both types enhance data processing and automation at the edge, facilitating real-time analysis and decision-making in industrial settings.</p></p>
