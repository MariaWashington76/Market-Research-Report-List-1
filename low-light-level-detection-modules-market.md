<p><p>Low-Light-Level Detection Modules are essential for capturing images in dim lighting conditions and split into two market types: Analog Modules and Digital Modules. Analog Modules process signals without digitization, offering real-time continuous output, making them suitable for applications like night vision. In contrast, Digital Modules convert light signals into digital data, enabling advanced processing and analysis capabilities. Digital Modules are often preferred for applications requiring high precision and integration with digital systems, while Analog Modules provide simplicity and speed for immediate use.</p></p>
