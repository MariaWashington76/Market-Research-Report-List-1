<p><p>The rubber anti-tack agents market includes various types such as stearates, fatty acid esters, fatty acid amides, and soaps. Stearates, like zinc stearate, provide excellent non-stick properties and lubrication. Fatty acid esters enhance durability and processing ease. Fatty acid amides offer moisture resistance and improved surface properties. Soaps, often made from fatty acids, act as effective release agents. Each type contributes to the processing and performance of rubber products by minimizing tackiness during manufacturing and application.</p></p>
