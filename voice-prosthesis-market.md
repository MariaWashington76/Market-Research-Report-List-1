<p><strong>The Voice Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indwelling Voice Prosthesis</li><li>Non-indwelling Voice Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voice prosthesis market is primarily categorized into indwelling and non-indwelling types. Indwelling voice prostheses are implantable devices that remain in place within the tracheoesophageal puncture and require minimal maintenance, facilitating easier speech for patients after laryngeal surgery. Non-indwelling voice prostheses, on the other hand, are removable and need regular positioning and care by the user or healthcare provider. Both types aim to restore speech functionality and improve quality of life for individuals with voice impairments.</p></p>
