<p><p>The casting market features two primary types of riser sleeves: insulation riser sleeves and fever riser sleeves. Insulation riser sleeves are designed to reduce heat loss during the solidification process, maintaining optimal temperature for molten metal and minimizing defects in the final cast. In contrast, fever riser sleeves help manage thermal gradients, enhancing the flow of molten metal and ensuring uniform solidification. Both types play crucial roles in improving casting quality and efficiency across various industrial applications.</p></p>
