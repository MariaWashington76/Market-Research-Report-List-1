<p><p>Business Performance Management (BPM) systems can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based BPM solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs by hosting the software on remote servers, facilitating easy access and collaboration. In contrast, on-premises BPM systems are installed locally, providing greater control and customization but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Organizations choose between these options based on their specific needs for security, cost, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
