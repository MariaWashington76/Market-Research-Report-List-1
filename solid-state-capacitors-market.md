<p><p>The solid-state capacitors market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Inorganic Dielectric Capacitors** use materials like ceramics or metal oxides, offering high stability and temperature resistance, ideal for power applications.  </p><p>2. **Organic Dielectric Capacitors** utilize organic materials, providing flexibility and lightweight characteristics, suitable for consumer electronics.  </p><p>3. **Electrolytic Capacitors** employ an electrolyte for high capacitance and efficiency, primarily used in power supply systems and audio equipment. Each type serves distinct applications based on their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
