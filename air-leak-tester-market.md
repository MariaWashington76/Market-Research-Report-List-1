<p><p>The air leak tester market comprises several types. Direct pressure leak detectors monitor pressure drops directly in a sealed system. Leak detectors for seals specifically test the integrity of seals and gaskets. Helium mass spectrometer leak detectors utilize helium's small atomic size for detecting minute leaks. Other market segments include specialized or niche leak detection technologies catering to specific applications.</p></p>
