<p><ul><li>Single-head Graphics Card</li><li>Multi-head Graphics Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical graphics card market is categorized into single-head and multi-head graphics cards. Single-head graphics cards are designed to support one display, making them suitable for basic imaging applications like ultrasound or X-rays in smaller healthcare settings. In contrast, multi-head graphics cards can drive multiple monitors simultaneously, enhancing the visualization of complex medical data and facilitating collaboration among medical professionals. These are ideal for advanced imaging systems such as MRI or CT scans, where detailed analysis is essential.</p></p>
