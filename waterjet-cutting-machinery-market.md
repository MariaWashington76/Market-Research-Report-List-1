<p><strong>The Waterjet Cutting Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet cutting machinery market comprises two primary types: pressurized water and abrasive mixture systems. Pressurized water systems utilize high-pressure jets of water to efficiently cut soft materials like rubber and foam. In contrast, abrasive mixture systems incorporate abrasive particles into the water stream, enabling the cutting of tougher materials such as metal, stone, and ceramics. Both types offer precision and versatility, catering to diverse industrial applications while ensuring minimal thermal distortion of the workpiece.</p></p>
