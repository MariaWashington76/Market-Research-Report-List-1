<p><p>The meso-micro porous carbon materials market is categorized into microporous and mesoporous materials based on pore size. Microporous materials, with pores less than 2 nm, are typically utilized in applications like gas storage and adsorption due to their high surface area and strong adsorption capabilities. In contrast, mesoporous materials, featuring pores ranging from 2 to 50 nm, are employed in catalysis and drug delivery due to their larger pore size, which allows for better diffusion and access to larger molecules.</p></p>
