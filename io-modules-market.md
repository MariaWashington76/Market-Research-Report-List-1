<p><p>IO modules serve as essential interfaces in automation systems, categorized into three main types: Analog modules, Digital modules, and Other modules. Analog modules process continuous signals, enabling precise control and monitoring of variables like temperature and pressure. Digital modules operate with discrete signals, essential for binary operations such as on/off switching. Other market segments may include specialized modules like communication interfaces or safety modules, enhancing system connectivity and compliance. Together, they ensure efficient data acquisition and control in industrial applications.</p></p>
