Additionally, PV embolization coils and plugs are specialized for portal vein interventions. Embolization particles and liquid embolics offer alternative methods for occlusion, while accessories enhance procedural efficiency and outcomes. Each device serves specific therapeutic needs in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
