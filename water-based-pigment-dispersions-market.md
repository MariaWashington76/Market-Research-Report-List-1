<p><p>Water-based pigment dispersions are primarily categorized into organic and inorganic types. Organic dispersions utilize carbon-based compounds, offering vibrant colors and excellent transparency, making them ideal for applications in coatings, paints, and inks. In contrast, inorganic dispersions consist of mineral-based pigments, providing superior stability, opacity, and durability in various environments. Both types are suitable for eco-friendly products due to their low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), catering to the growing demand for sustainable and safe materials in various industries.</p></p>
