<p><ul><li>Silical Sol Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li><li>Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pump casting market encompasses various techniques for fabricating pump components. Silica sol casting utilizes a water-soluble silica binder, offering high precision and a smooth finish, ideal for complex shapes. Sand casting, a traditional method, uses sand molds and is cost-effective for large parts but may lack dimensional precision. Die casting, employing molten metal injected into reusable molds, excels in producing lightweight, durable components with excellent surface quality, making it suitable for high-volume production in diverse applications.</p></p>
