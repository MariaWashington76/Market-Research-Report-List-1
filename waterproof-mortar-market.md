<p><p>The waterproof mortar market comprises two main types: ordinary waterproof mortar and special waterproof mortar. Ordinary waterproof mortar is designed for general applications, providing basic moisture resistance suitable for standard construction needs. In contrast, special waterproof mortar is formulated for specific environments, offering enhanced properties such as chemical resistance, flexibility, or high durability. This type is ideal for demanding applications like swimming pools, basements, and industrial settings where conventional mortars may fail to protect against water infiltration effectively.</p></p>
