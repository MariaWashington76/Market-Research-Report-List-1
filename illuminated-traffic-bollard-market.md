<p><p>The Illuminated Traffic Bollard Market includes two main types: LED and Conventional. They are ideal for modern urban designs and eco-friendly projects. Conventional bollards rely on traditional lighting like halogen or incandescent bulbs, which are less energy-efficient and have shorter lifespans but may be preferred for specific aesthetic or cost reasons.
