<p><ul><li>Liquid Sealing Coatings</li><li>Dry Sealing Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sealing coatings market is primarily divided into two types: liquid sealing coatings and dry sealing coatings. Liquid sealing coatings are applied in a wet state, forming a protective layer upon drying, ideal for waterproofing and corrosion resistance. They often provide flexibility and adhesion to various surfaces. In contrast, dry sealing coatings are pre-applied coatings that offer immediate sealing capabilities, typically requiring no additional drying time. Both types serve crucial roles in industries like construction, automotive, and electronics to enhance durability and performance.</p></p>
