<p><p>The bone densitometer market comprises primarily two types: DEXA (Dual Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and ultrasound devices. DEXA is the gold standard for measuring bone mineral density, using low dose X-rays to assess bone health and diagnose osteoporosis. In contrast, ultrasound systems use sound waves to evaluate bone density, offering a radiation-free alternative that is portable and easier to use. Both technologies cater to diverse healthcare environments, enhancing the diagnosis and management of bone-related conditions.</p></p>
