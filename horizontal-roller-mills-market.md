<p><ul><li>Double Rollers</li><li>Four Rollers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Horizontal Roller Mills market features two primary configurations: double rollers and four rollers. Double roller mills utilize two adjacent rollers to crush and grind materials, offering a compact design suited for smaller operations. In contrast, four roller mills employ an additional set of rollers for enhanced grinding efficiency and finer particle size reduction, making them suitable for larger-scale production. Both types cater to various industries, including food processing, minerals, and chemicals, emphasizing efficiency and improved output quality.</p></p>
