<p><strong>The Optically Clear Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Glass</li><li>Rolled Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optically clear glass market includes various types, primarily float glass and rolled glass. Float glass, known for its smooth surface and clarity, is produced by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in uniform thickness and minimal distortion. It is widely used in windows and mirrors. In contrast, rolled glass is created by passing molten glass through rollers, allowing for different textures and thicknesses. This type is often used in applications requiring strength and aesthetics, such as architectural elements.</p></p>
