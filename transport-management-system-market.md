<p><p>The Transport Management System (TMS) market encompasses various types aimed at optimizing logistics operations. Transport sourcing involves selecting carriers and negotiating rates, while capacity management focuses on utilizing transport assets efficiently. Performance management tracks and analyzes delivery metrics, ensuring service standards are met. Network design addresses the layout of distribution and transport routes, enhancing efficiency. Shipping consolidation combines multiple shipments to reduce costs, and route planning optimizes paths for timely and cost-effective deliveries, ultimately improving supply chain performance.</p></p>
