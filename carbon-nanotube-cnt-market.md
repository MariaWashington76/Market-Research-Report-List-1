<p><p>The carbon nanotube (CNT) market is primarily divided into two types: single-walled carbon nanotubes (SWCNTs) and multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs). SWCNTs consist of a single cylindrical layer, offering unique electrical and mechanical properties, ideal for applications in electronics and nanotechnology. MWCNTs consist of multiple layers, providing enhanced strength and conductivity, making them suitable for composite materials and energy storage. Both types are integral to advancements in materials science, electronics, and nanotechnology applications globally.</p></p>
