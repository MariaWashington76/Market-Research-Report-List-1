<p><strong>The Hydrogen Circulation Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roots</li><li>Claw</li><li>Scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen circulation pump market includes three main types: Roots, Claw, and Scroll pumps. Roots pumps utilize a pair of rotors to move hydrogen efficiently, making them suitable for high flow rates. Claw pumps use two rotating claws to compress and move the gas with minimal contact, ensuring lower maintenance needs. Scroll pumps utilize spiral elements to compress hydrogen quietly and efficiently, offering compact designs and high reliability. Each type caters to different industrial applications based on efficiency, capacity, and operational requirements.</p></p>
