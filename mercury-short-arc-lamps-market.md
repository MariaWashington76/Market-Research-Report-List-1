<p><p>The Mercury Short Arc Lamps market is primarily divided into two types: DC Mercury Short Arc Lamps and AC Mercury Short Arc Lamps. DC Mercury Short Arc Lamps utilize direct current to produce a stable, luminous arc, offering consistent brightness and color quality, making them ideal for applications like cinema projection and specialized industrial uses. In contrast, AC Mercury Short Arc Lamps operate on alternating current, providing variations in light output and energy efficiency, commonly used in general lighting and outdoor applications.</p></p>
