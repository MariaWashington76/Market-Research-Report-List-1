<p><p>The Mineral Insulated Resistance Thermometer (RTD) market is categorized mainly into two types based on conductor configurations: Simplex and Duplex conductors. Simplex conductors consist of a single sensing element, making them cost-effective and suitable for non-critical applications. In contrast, Duplex conductors feature two sensing elements, enhancing accuracy and reliability by allowing for redundancy or simultaneous measurement, ideal for demanding industrial environments. Both types are essential for precise temperature monitoring in various sectors, including manufacturing and energy.</p></p>
