<p><p>The irrigation pumps market is categorized into two main types: submersible and non-submersible pumps. Submersible pumps operate underwater, effectively drawing water from deep sources, making them suitable for deep wells and boreholes. They are efficient and have a compact design. Non-submersible pumps, on the other hand, are installed above the water source and rely on suction to function. They are commonly used for surface water applications. Both types serve critical roles in optimizing water usage for agricultural purposes.</p></p>
