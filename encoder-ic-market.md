<p><ul><li>Linear Encoder IC</li><li>Rotary Encoder IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The encoder IC market is primarily categorized into linear and rotary encoder ICs. Linear encoder ICs convert position data into electrical signals along a straight path, commonly used in applications like 3D printers and CNC machines. In contrast, rotary encoder ICs are designed to capture rotational position and speed, widely utilized in robotics, automotive systems, and industrial automation. Both types are essential for precise motion control in various sectors, driving demand for advancements in accuracy and integration capabilities.</p></p>
