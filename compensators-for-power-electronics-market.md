<p><strong>The Compensators For Power Electronics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC)</li><li>Thyristor-Switched Series Capacitor (TSSC)</li><li>Thyristor-Switched Series Reactor (TSSR)</li><li>Thyristor-Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC)</li><li>Thyristor-Controlled Series Reactor (TCSR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compensators in the power electronics market enhance voltage stability and power flow in transmission systems. The Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC) uses a voltage source inverter to control power flow dynamically. Thyristor-Switched Series Capacitors (TSSC) and Thyristor-Switched Series Reactors (TSSR) offer fixed compensation by switching capacitors or reactors for reactive power management. The Thyristor-Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) allows for variable compensation by adjusting the capacitance, while the Thyristor-Controlled Series Reactor (TCSR) offers similar flexibility with inductive loads.</p></p>
