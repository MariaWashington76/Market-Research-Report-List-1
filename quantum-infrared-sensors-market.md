<p><p>The quantum infrared sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: photon detection quantum infrared sensors and thermal detection quantum infrared sensors. Photon detection sensors operate by detecting individual photons in the infrared spectrum, offering high sensitivity and precision, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed imaging and spectroscopy. In contrast, thermal detection sensors measure temperature changes due to absorbed infrared radiation, providing broad-spectrum detection for applications like surveillance and environmental monitoring. Both types enhance capabilities across various industries.</p></p>
