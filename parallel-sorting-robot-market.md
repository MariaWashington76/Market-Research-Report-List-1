<p><p>The Parallel Sorting Robot market can be categorized into two main types: Two-Axis and Multi-Axis systems. Two-Axis robots operate along two primary movements, typically in X and Y directions, making them suitable for simpler sorting tasks with limited space. In contrast, Multi-Axis robots feature additional degrees of movement, allowing for more complex sorting operations in three-dimensional environments. This adaptability enables Multi-Axis robots to handle diverse product shapes and sizes, enhancing efficiency in high-capacity sorting applications across various industries.</p></p>
