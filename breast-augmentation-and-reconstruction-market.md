Tissue expanders are temporary implants used in reconstructive surgery to gradually stretch the skin and tissue, creating enough space for permanent implants. Both options cater to diverse aesthetic and medical needs, contributing to the growing demand for breast augmentation and reconstruction procedures.</p></p>
