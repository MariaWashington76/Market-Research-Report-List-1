<p><strong>The Thermal Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Closed Type</li><li>Normally Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal actuator market is primarily categorized into Normally Closed (NC) and Normally Open (NO) types. Normally Closed thermal actuators remain shut when inactive, opening in response to temperature increases, making them suitable for applications needing fail-safe conditions. Conversely, Normally Open thermal actuators are open when inactive and close upon heating, ideal for controlling scenarios where immediate activation is required. The choice between these types depends on the specific application needs, such as safety, energy efficiency, and operational requirements.</p></p>
