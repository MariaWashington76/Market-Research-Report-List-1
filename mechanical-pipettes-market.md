<p><ul><li>Single-Channel Pipette</li><li>Multi-Channel Pipette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical pipettes market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel and multi-channel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed for transferring individual samples and offer precision for various liquid handling tasks. In contrast, multi-channel pipettes allow simultaneous transfer of liquids across multiple channels, enhancing efficiency, especially in high-throughput environments such as research and clinical laboratories. Both types cater to specific laboratory needs, with single-channel pipettes focusing on accuracy and multi-channel pipettes prioritizing speed and volume consistency.</p></p>
