<p><strong>The Automotive Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Power Steering (EPS)</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li><li>Electronic & Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive power steering systems market comprises three main types: Electronic Power Steering (EPS), Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), and Electronic & Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). EPS uses electric motors for steering assistance, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing vehicle weight. HPS relies on hydraulic fluid pressure generated by a pump, offering a traditional feel but less efficiency. EHPS combines both systems, utilizing electric assistance while retaining hydraulic functionality, providing a balance of performance and efficiency suitable for various vehicle types.</p></p>
