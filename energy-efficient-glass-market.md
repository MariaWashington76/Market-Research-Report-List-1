<p><p>The energy-efficient glass market is primarily segmented into two types: hard coat and soft coat. Hard coat glass features a durable, transparent coating that reflects heat while allowing natural light, making it ideal for construction and automotive applications. Soft coat glass, in contrast, has a thinner, more reflective coating that enhances thermal insulation and light transmission, suitable for residential and commercial buildings. Both types contribute to energy savings and improved comfort by optimizing heating and cooling performance.</p></p>
