<p><p>The access cards market is primarily divided into three types: proximity cards, smart cards, and others. Proximity cards use RFID technology to grant access without direct contact, making them ideal for quick entry. Smart cards are equipped with microprocessors that enable secure data storage and encryption, offering enhanced security features. Each type serves distinct applications in security, identification, and access control across various industries.</p></p>
