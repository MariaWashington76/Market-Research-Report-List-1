The market is divided into two main types: Linear and Non-linear. Linear FVDs provide consistent damping force proportional to the velocity of movement, ensuring predictable performance. In contrast, Non-linear FVDs exhibit variable damping characteristics, allowing for tailored responses to different loading conditions, making them suitable for applications requiring adaptive performance under varying dynamic scenarios.</p></p>
