<p><p>The LNG liquefaction equipment market is primarily categorized into heat exchangers and compressors. Heat exchangers facilitate the cooling process by transferring heat away from natural gas, enabling its liquefaction at low temperatures. Compressors, on the other hand, increase the pressure of the gas to aid in its transition to a liquid state. Both components are critical in ensuring efficient energy conversion and storage, catering to the growing demand for liquefied natural gas in global energy markets.</p></p>
