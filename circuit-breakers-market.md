<p><p>Circuit breakers serve as essential safety devices in electrical systems, functioning to interrupt current flow in fault conditions. The market includes various types: Air Circuit Breakers utilize air for arc extinction, Oil Circuit Breakers rely on oil for insulation and cooling, Sulfur Hexafluoride Circuit Breakers use SF6 gas for its excellent insulating properties, and Vacuum Circuit Breakers contain contacts in a vacuum for quick arc extinction. Additional circuit breakers cater to specific applications, enhancing system reliability and safety.</p></p>
