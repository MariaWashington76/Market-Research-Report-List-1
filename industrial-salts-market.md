<p><p>The industrial salts market mainly comprises two types: brine and salt mined from underground deposits. Brine is a solution of salt in water, typically used for de-icing, chemical production, and food preservation; it is obtained through evaporation or natural saline water sources. In contrast, salt mined from deposits involves extracting solid salt from underground layers via traditional mining methods. Both types cater to various industries, including chemical manufacturing, agriculture, and food processing, driving demand and market growth.</p></p>
