<p><p>Elastomeric couplers are crucial components in various mechanical applications, categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. Jaw type couplers feature a flexible elastomer between two rigid jaws, providing shock absorption. Gear type couplers utilize gears to connect shafts while allowing for misalignment. Tire type couplers consist of a rubber tire element that enhances flexibility and dampening. Dowel pin type couplers utilize pins for alignment while accommodating some flex. Other types may include custom designs for specific applications.</p></p>
