<p><p>The Civil Infrared Thermal Imager market is primarily segmented into two types: uncooled infrared thermal imagers and refrigeration infrared thermal imagers. Uncooled infrared thermal imagers operate without the need for cooling systems, making them more compact and energy-efficient, suitable for a variety of applications such as building inspections and surveillance. In contrast, refrigeration infrared thermal imagers utilize cooling technology to enhance sensitivity and precision, often employed in demanding environments like industrial inspections and scientific research. Each type offers distinct advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
