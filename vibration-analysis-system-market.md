<p><p>The vibration analysis system market is segmented into online and offline systems. Online vibration analysis systems continuously monitor machinery conditions in real-time, enabling prompt detection of issues and minimizing downtime. These systems are typically integrated with plant control systems for seamless data collection and analysis. In contrast, offline vibration analysis systems involve periodic data collection using portable devices, allowing for detailed examination and troubleshooting of equipment during scheduled maintenance. Both types are essential for predictive maintenance and enhancing equipment reliability.</p></p>
