<p><ul><li>Uncooled Thermal Imaging Camera</li><li>Cooled Thermal Imaging Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal imaging camera market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal imaging cameras. Uncooled thermal imaging cameras operate without the need for cooling systems, making them lighter, more compact, and cost-effective, ideal for various applications like surveillance and automotive diagnostics. In contrast, cooled thermal imaging cameras utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and image quality, typically used in demanding environments such as military and aerospace. Both types serve crucial roles across industrial, commercial, and security sectors.</p></p>
