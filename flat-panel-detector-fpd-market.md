<p><p>Flat Panel Detectors (FPD) are advanced imaging devices used in medical and industrial applications to capture high-quality digital radiography images. The market is divided into two main types: Indirect FPDs, which convert X-rays into visible light before capturing the image using a photodetector, and Direct FPDs, which directly convert X-rays into electrical signals without an intermediate step. Both types offer benefits such as improved image quality, reduced exposure to radiation, and faster processing times, catering to varying imaging needs and technologies.</p></p>
