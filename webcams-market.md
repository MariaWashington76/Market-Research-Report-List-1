<p><p>The webcams market can be categorized into two main types: USB port webcams and wireless webcams. USB port webcams connect directly to a computer via USB cables, offering reliable performance with minimal latency, making them ideal for stationary use. In contrast, wireless webcams operate via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, providing greater flexibility and mobility for users. This type is popular for remote conferencing and streaming, as it eliminates cable clutter and allows for easy repositioning, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
