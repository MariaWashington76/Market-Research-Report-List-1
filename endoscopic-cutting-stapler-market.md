<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Standard</li><li>Circular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscopic cutting stapler market is categorized into three primary types: linear, standard, and circular. Linear staplers are designed for straight cuts and are often used in procedures requiring tissue resection. Standard staplers are versatile and can handle various surgical needs, suitable for both open and minimally invasive surgeries. Circular staplers, on the other hand, are utilized for anastomosis, creating circular staples for connecting tissue segments. Each type addresses specific surgical requirements, enhancing procedural efficiency and outcomes.</p></p>
