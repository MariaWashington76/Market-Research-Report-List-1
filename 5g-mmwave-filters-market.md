<p><strong>The 5G mmWave Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LTCC 5G mmWave Filters</li><li>SAW 5G mmWave Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G mmWave filters market includes two primary types: LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) filters and SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) filters. LTCC filters are known for their high integration, low losses, and ability to operate in compact packages, making them suitable for various 5G applications. In contrast, SAW filters offer excellent frequency selectivity and stability at higher frequencies but may have limitations in size and integration compared to LTCC. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing 5G communication efficiency.</p></p>
