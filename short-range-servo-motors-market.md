<p><p>The short-range servo motors market comprises primarily two types: 2-phase hybrid stepper motors and 3-phase hybrid stepper motors. 2-phase hybrid stepper motors are known for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, utilizing two windings to achieve precise position control, making them suitable for various applications in automation and robotics. In contrast, 3-phase hybrid stepper motors provide smoother operation and enhanced torque at higher speeds, making them ideal for demanding tasks in industries like CNC machining and 3D printing.</p></p>
