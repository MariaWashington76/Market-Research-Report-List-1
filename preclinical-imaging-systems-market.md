<p><p>The preclinical imaging systems market features various modalities, each serving vital research needs. MRI provides high-resolution soft tissue imaging. PET enables visualization of metabolic processes using radiotracers, while SPECT offers insights into functional activity through gamma-ray emissions. Micro-CT delivers detailed 3D anatomical structures at a micro-scale. Optical imaging provides real-time observations of biological processes, and Magnetic Particle Imaging (MPI) offers high-speed imaging with superior resolution and no ionizing radiation. Together, these technologies enhance preclinical research and drug development.</p></p>
