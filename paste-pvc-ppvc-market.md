<p><p>The Paste PVC (PPVC) market is categorized into two main types: homogeneous and heterogeneous. The homogeneous type consists of uniform particle sizes and composition, providing consistent properties and applications in products like pipes and films. In contrast, the heterogeneous type features a mix of different particle sizes and compositions, which can enhance flexibility and performance in specific applications, such as coatings and adhesives. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs based on their distinct material characteristics.</p></p>
