<p><p>Non-thermal processing methods are innovative technologies that preserve food quality while extending shelf life. High Pressure Processing (HPP) uses intense pressure to inactivate pathogens and spoilage organisms. Pulsed Electric Field (PEF) applies short bursts of electricity to disrupt cell membranes, enhancing extraction and preservation. Irradiation employs ionizing radiation to eliminate microorganisms. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves to achieve food preservation through cavitation effects. Cold Plasma incorporates ionized gases to achieve microbial reduction while maintaining food integrity, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
