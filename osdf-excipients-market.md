<p><p>The OSDF excipients market comprises various forms used in drug formulation. Powders are fine particles that enhance solubility and stability. Granules are larger aggregates that improve flowability and compressibility. Tablets are solid dosage forms created by compressing powders or granules, ensuring accurate dosage and extended release. Capsules consist of gelatin shells filled with powders or granules, offering easy swallowing and taste masking. Dropping pills are innovative delivery systems that dissolve quickly for rapid absorption. Each type plays a crucial role in drug efficacy and patient compliance.</p></p>
