<p><strong>The Olefin Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Hollow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The olefin fiber market is primarily segmented into solid and hollow types, each serving distinct applications. Solid olefin fibers are known for their durability, lightweight nature, and resistance to moisture, making them ideal for outdoor fabrics, upholstery, and industrial applications. In contrast, hollow olefin fibers have an air-filled structure that enhances insulation properties, making them suitable for thermal insulation, inner linings, and functional clothing. Both types cater to diverse industries, including textiles, automotive, and home furnishings.</p></p>
