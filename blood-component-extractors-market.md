<p><p>The blood component extractors market is segmented into two primary types: intermittent type and continuous type. Intermittent type blood component extractors operate in cycles, collecting specific blood components at designated intervals, making them suitable for smaller facilities or targeted collections. In contrast, continuous type extractors provide a steady, uninterrupted flow of blood processing, allowing for real-time separation and extraction of components, ideal for larger facilities or higher-volume blood processing needs, ensuring efficiency and maximizing yield.</p></p>
