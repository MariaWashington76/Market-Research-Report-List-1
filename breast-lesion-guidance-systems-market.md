Magnetic tracers utilize magnetic materials to facilitate lesion identification through imaging techniques. Electromagnetic reflectors employ electromagnetic fields to pinpoint abnormalities non-invasively. Other market types may include technologies such as ultrasound-guided systems or radioactive seed localization, each offering unique advantages for accurate breast lesion management and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
