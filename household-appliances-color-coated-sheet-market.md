PCM involves a base metal sheet that is coated with a polymer coating, providing durability and aesthetic appeal for appliances. VCM, on the other hand, features a vinyl coating that offers enhanced resistance to moisture and chemicals, making it suitable for various household applications. Both types are designed to improve the visual and functional quality of appliances while ensuring longevity.</p></p>
