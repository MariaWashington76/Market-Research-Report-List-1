<p><ul><li>PEX-A</li><li>PEX-B</li><li>PEX-C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PEX pipe market features three primary types: PEX-A, PEX-B, and PEX-C, differentiated by their manufacturing processes. PEX-A, produced through the Engel method, offers superior flexibility and resistance to kinking. PEX-B, made via the dry method, ensures higher thermal stability and is more cost-effective, making it popular in residential applications. PEX-C, created using irradiation, boasts good resistance to chemicals and high-pressure environments. Each type caters to specific plumbing and heating needs, influencing market trends and consumer preferences.</p></p>
