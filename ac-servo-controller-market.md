<p><ul><li>Uniaxial</li><li>Multiaxial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC servo controller market is divided into two primary types: uniaxial and multiaxial. Uniaxial controllers manage a single axis, making them ideal for applications requiring precise motion control in one direction, such as robotics or single-axis CNC machines. In contrast, multiaxial controllers operate multiple axes simultaneously, essential for complex tasks involving coordinated movements, such as in automation and advanced manufacturing systems. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in various industrial applications.</p></p>
