<p><ul><li>RFID Based</li><li>UWB Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Forklift Pedestrian Warning System market is primarily divided into RFID-based and UWB-based solutions. RFID-based systems utilize radio frequency identification tags attached to pedestrians and forklifts, enabling detection and communication to prevent collisions. In contrast, UWB-based systems use ultra-wideband technology for precise location tracking, providing enhanced accuracy and real-time alerts. Both types aim to improve safety in industrial environments by establishing awareness between moving forklifts and nearby pedestrians, thus reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
