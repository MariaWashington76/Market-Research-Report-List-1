<p><strong>The Automotive Corner Sonar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Sonar</li><li>Active Sonar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive corner sonar market consists of two main types: passive and active sonar systems. Passive sonar relies on detecting sound waves emitted from external sources, such as nearby objects, helping the vehicle gauge its surroundings without sending out signals. In contrast, active sonar systems emit sound pulses and analyze the echoes returning from objects, providing precise distance and movement information. Both systems enhance vehicle safety and assist with parking and obstacle detection, contributing to the overall advancement of automotive technology.</p></p>
