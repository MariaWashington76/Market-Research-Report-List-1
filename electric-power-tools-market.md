<p><p>The electric power tools market is primarily divided into two types: corded and cordless tools. Corded power tools are powered through an electrical outlet, offering consistent power and generally higher performance for heavy-duty tasks. They are ideal for stationary work where mobility is less of an issue. In contrast, cordless power tools operate on rechargeable batteries, providing greater portability and convenience for tasks in diverse locations. However, they may have limited runtime and lower power output compared to their corded counterparts.</p></p>
