<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostics</li><li>Molecular Diagnostics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRSA testing systems market encompasses two primary types: immunodiagnostics and molecular diagnostics. Immunodiagnostic methods utilize antibodies to detect MRSA antigens in samples, providing rapid results for infection diagnosis. In contrast, molecular diagnostics involve techniques like PCR (polymerase chain reaction) to identify MRSA's genetic material, ensuring high specificity and sensitivity. Both types are crucial for effective infection control, guiding appropriate antibiotic therapy, and reducing the spread of MRSA in healthcare settings. Each method offers unique advantages in clinical applications.</p></p>
