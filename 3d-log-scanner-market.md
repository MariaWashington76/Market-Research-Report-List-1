<p><p>The 3D log scanner market primarily includes laser and X-ray scanning technologies. Laser scanners use high-resolution laser beams to capture detailed surface profiles of logs, offering precision in measurement and wood quality assessment. In contrast, X-ray scanners utilize penetrating radiation to analyze the internal structure of logs, identifying defects and optimizing yield. Both technologies enhance efficiency in the lumber industry by providing accurate data for processing, improving product quality, and facilitating better resource management.</p></p>
