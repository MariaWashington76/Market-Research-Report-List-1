<p><p>The metallic spray paint market is segmented into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic spray paints use organic solvents as carriers, offering durability, fast drying, and high gloss finishes, making them ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Water-based metallic spray paints, on the other hand, use water as a carrier, providing eco-friendly, low-VOC options with easier cleanup and reduced environmental impact, suitable for indoor and consumer-focused projects. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing performance and sustainability.</p></p>
