<p><ul><li>Granular Urea</li><li>Prilled Urea</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agriculture urea market primarily includes two types: granular urea and prilled urea. Granular urea consists of larger, uniform granules, making it suitable for bulk application and offering slow dissolution, which enhances nutrient availability over time. In contrast, prilled urea features smaller, round pellets that dissolve quickly, providing immediate nitrogen release for crops. Both types serve essential roles in fertilization, catering to different agricultural practices and preferences for nitrogen delivery efficiency, influencing their market dynamics and usage.</p></p>
