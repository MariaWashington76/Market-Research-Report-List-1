<p><p>The 3D display market encompasses various technologies, including Digital Light Processing (DLP), Plasma Display Panel (PDP), Organic Light-Emitting Diode (OLED), and Light Emitting Diode (LED). DLP uses micro-mirrors to project images, offering high brightness and sharpness. PDP utilizes gas-discharge technology for vibrant colors and deep blacks. OLED displays provide superior contrast and flexibility due to self-emissive pixels. LED displays, commonly used in backlighting, offer energy efficiency and brightness, making them popular for both residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
