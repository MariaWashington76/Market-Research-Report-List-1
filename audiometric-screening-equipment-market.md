<p><ul><li>OAE Screening Equipment</li><li>ABR Screening Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audiometric screening equipment market includes various types, primarily Otoacoustic Emission (OAE) screening equipment and Auditory Brainstem Response (ABR) screening equipment. OAE screening equipment assesses inner ear functionality by measuring sound waves, helping identify hearing loss in newborns and children. ABR screening equipment evaluates the auditory nerve's response to sound, often used for more comprehensive assessments in both infants and adults. Together, these technologies play a crucial role in early detection and intervention of hearing impairments.</p></p>
