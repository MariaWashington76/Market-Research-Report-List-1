<p><p>A coated stent graft is a medical device used to treat vascular conditions, particularly in the management of aneurysms. It consists of a stent, which provides structural support to blood vessels, covered with a graft material that promotes healing and reduces the risk of complications, such as restenosis and thrombosis. The coating often contains bioactive agents that enhance the biocompatibility of the device.</p><p>The coated stent graft market is experiencing significant growth driven by the increasing prevalence of vascular diseases, technological advancements, and rising aging populations.
