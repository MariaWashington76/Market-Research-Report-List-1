<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor grade encapsulants market consists of two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component encapsulants are pre-mixed and ready to use, offering convenience and ease of application, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, two-component encapsulants require mixing of a resin and hardener before application, allowing for customizable curing times and properties, which can enhance performance in specific applications. Both types play critical roles in protecting electronic components from environmental stressors.</p></p>
