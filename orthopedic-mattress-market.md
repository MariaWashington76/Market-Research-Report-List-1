<p><ul><li>Spring Orthopedic Mattress</li><li>Non-Spring Orthopedic Mattress</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic mattress market is primarily categorized into two types: spring and non-spring orthopedic mattresses. Spring orthopedic mattresses utilize a network of coils to provide support, promoting spinal alignment and reducing pressure points. They are known for their durability and breathability. In contrast, non-spring orthopedic mattresses include memory foam and latex options, which conform closely to the body, offering excellent contouring and support. Both types aim to enhance sleep quality and alleviate discomfort, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
