<p><p>The sealed gauge transducer market is categorized into three types based on pressure levels: low pressure sealed, mid pressure sealed, and high pressure sealed transducers. Low pressure sealed transducers are designed for applications with pressures ranging up to 100 psi, typically used in HVAC and food processing. Mid pressure sealed transducers operate between 100 and 1,000 psi, suitable for industrial automation. High pressure sealed transducers exceed 1,000 psi, often applied in oil and gas, aerospace, and hydraulic systems, ensuring high precision and durability.</p></p>
